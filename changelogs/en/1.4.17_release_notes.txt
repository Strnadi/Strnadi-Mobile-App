Google Play Console (EN, short version)
---------------------------------------
We improved map recording playback and download progress. Download progress across parts is now accurate (no false 100% or jumps), playback progress is more precise, seeking is limited to valid bounds, and progress bars are easier to read. We also made ongoing updates to sign-in, localization, and privacy settings.


App Store Connect (EN, detailed version)
----------------------------------------
In this release, we focused on stability and progress accuracy for recording download and playback:

- Fixed multi-part recording download progress on the map to prevent jumps and false 100% states.
- Improved playback progress behavior (more accurate duration fallback, edge-state handling, correct behavior after reaching the end).
- Limited playback seek actions to valid time bounds.
- Improved progress bar readability with clearer track/fill colors.

We also included ongoing improvements in sign-in flows, localization, and privacy settings.
